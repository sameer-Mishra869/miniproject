Frequently Asked Questions

Where and when will the 2026 Olympic Winter Games take place?

+

The 2026 Olympic Winter Games will take place from February 6-22, 2026 in Milano Cortina, Italy. Events will be held across multiple venues in Milan, Cortina d'Ampezzo, Bormio, Livigno, and other locations in Northern Italy.

How to get tickets for the Winter Olympics 2026?

+

Tickets for Milano Cortina 2026 can be purchased through the official Olympics website and authorized ticket resellers. Various ticket packages are available for different events and ceremonies.

How many countries will participate in the Winter Olympics 2026?

+

Approximately 95 countries are expected to participate in the Milano Cortina 2026 Winter Olympics, with over 2,900 athletes competing across 15 sports and 116 events.

What is the mascot of the Winter Olympics 2026?

+

The mascots of Milano Cortina 2026 are Tina and Milo - two adorable stoats representing the spirit of the Italian Alps. They embody energy, passion, and the love for winter sports.

What sports are in the Olympic Winter Games Milano Cortina 2026?

+

The Games will feature 15 sports: Alpine Skiing, Biathlon, Bobsled, Cross-Country Skiing, Curling, Figure Skating, Freestyle Skiing, Ice Hockey, Luge, Nordic Combined, Short Track Speed Skating, Skeleton, Ski Jumping, Snowboard, and Speed Skating.

Where to watch the Olympic Winter Games Milano Cortina 2026?

+

You can watch the Games on the Olympic Channel, official broadcast partners in your country, and through the Olympics app for live streaming and highlights.
